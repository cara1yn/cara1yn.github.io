A designer with 4+ years of experience in branding, typography, visual identity, UX/UI design, and information architecture. Previous work includes Burnett Group, Shareblue, and the American Culinary Federation. I earned my BA in Graphic Design from Flagler College in 2014 and certification in Human-Centered Design from IDEO in 2016. Currently a member and volunteer of AIGA NY, an ad-hoc volunteer designer at SURJ NYC Chapter and Murray Hill Neighborhood Association, and a design mentor for Black Girls Code.
