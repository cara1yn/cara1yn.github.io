My Role

My role was to work with Bridge's product team, engineers, users and stakeholders to figure out what improvements could be made to Bridge. I worked most closely with product to determine which items on the roadmap needed the most attention and which were attainable by sprint and quarterly. Collaboratively with product, I conducted user interviews and A/B testing on new designs we were trialing. Later on, we added more UX designers and researchers to the team, at which point our focus shifted to beginning the development of a Material Design library. .
