Overview

Creating a magazine showcasing 35mm film photographs. Featured as an Editor's Pick on Issuu. Student work.

Challenge

I challenged myself to find a way of blending old and new work cohesively in a single project. I wanted to preserve these photos and save them from a life of unseen scans on unused harddrives so I included them in the work.

Process & Solution

From 2008-2013 I carried a Minolta almost everywhere with me and took photos of everyday life. Years later I revisited these scans and arranged them into an issue celebrating the "Ordinary". Conceptually, the magazine would have a different featured theme every month and run prints of the audience's photo submissions based on the subject.

My Role

Conceptualization, photography, layout, design, typography.
